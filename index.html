Connecting Forces - Ultra Reliable mobile multichannel Broadband connection


We ensure data flow in demanding conditions, where always online connectivity is critical. We offer reliable, operator-independent, cost-effective connection for mobile and fixed environments.


Goodmill Systems Ltd develops, manufactures, and sells multichannel routers. These routers are vehicle-mounted and provide broadband connectivity to various user devices in vehicles.


The multichannel feature allows the router to combine multiple separate wireless broadband networks into a single, logical network connection. This combined network offers enhanced coverage, reliability, and to some extent, capacity, leveraging the strengths of each connected network. The networks used can be private or public and can include any wireless broadband technology such as 3G, LTE, 5G, Wi-Fi, or satellite.
